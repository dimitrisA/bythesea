Viral taxonomy. Some mnemonics

DNA Viruses Generally: ds DNA, icosahedral symmetry, replicate in the nucleus (where DNA is normally found). Of them: Three viruses are naked. Papova, Adeno, Parvo (“Woman naked for PAP smear exam”). Parvo is so simple it has a single DNA strand. SS Linear DNA. It’s also naked hence simple. “One Par Golf”. “A golf bat is linear and single stranded”. (22nm only, one of the smallest). “Pox in a box”.
